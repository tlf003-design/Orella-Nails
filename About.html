About ORELLA Nail Studio

ORELLA Nail Studio is a luxury nail salon dedicated to creativity, beauty, and care. We believe that nails are not just an accessory, but a form of self-expression that deserves artistry and attention to detail.

Our mission is to bring beauty and confidence to every visitor by offering premium services in a relaxing, welcoming environment. Whether you’re looking for elegant nail extensions, vibrant nail art, or soothing treatments, we tailor every session to your unique style.
